## HA Sketchbook Cards

A collection of **29** hand-drawn, sketchbook-style custom cards for Home Assistant dashboards.

Inspired by [sketchbook-ui](https://github.com/SarthakRawat-1/sketchbook-ui), these cards bring a unique notebook aesthetic to your smart home dashboard with:

- **Hand-drawn SVG borders** — wobbly double-stroke rounded borders unique per entity
- **Paper texture** — feTurbulence noise grain for a real paper feel
- **Caveat + Patrick Hand** handwriting fonts
- **Active state tint** — cards glow with accent color when entities are on
- **Configurable corners** — 0 (sharp) to 30 (pill) via slider
- **Card variants** — paper, notebook (ruled lines), sticky note (tape strip)
- **Dark mode** — auto-detects and adjusts shadows/colors
- **Visual editors** — every card has a full ha-form editor

### Cards Included

**Core Cards:**
Entity, Button, Light, Thermostat, Weather, Sensor, Media Player, Cover, Alarm Panel, Clock

**Mobile Cards:**
Chip, Person, Tile, Camera

**Navigation & Layout:**
Pop-up, Horizontal Buttons Stack, Sub-Button, Separator

**Utility Cards:**
Fan, Lock, Number

**New in v1.4:**
- **Template Card** — Jinja2 templates for dynamic text, icons, colors
- **History Graph Card** — Multi-entity sparklines with color thresholds
- **Room Card** — Room summary with occupancy + sensor readouts
- **Select Card** — Dropdown picker for input_select entities
- **Progress Card** — Radial progress ring with hand-drawn wobble
- **Timeline Card** — Activity journal from HA logbook
- **TOG Card** — Baby sleep recommendation with clothing illustrations
- **Step Battle Card** — Fitness competition with VS layout, progress bars, 7-day chart

All cards integrate with Home Assistant themes, support card-mod styling, and respect your dashboard color settings.
